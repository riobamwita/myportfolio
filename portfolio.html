Air Pollution Exposure Mapping

Mapping and analysis of population exposure to air pollutants by integrating emission sources, land use patterns, meteorological data, and settlement distribution.

The map shows that the central part of Nakuru County, particularly around the town center, exhibits high to very high exposure levels (red to purple), with PM2.5 concentrations exceeding approximately 36–48 µg/m³ and NO₂ levels above 15–20 µmol/m².

6

Urban Heat Island Analysis

Assessment of temperature variations within urban environments using remote sensing and spatial analysis.

The Land Surface Temperature (LST) map for the Winam Gulf region, focusing on Kisumu City, shows intense urban heat compared to surrounding rural areas. The mean urban temperature was 41.9°C versus 34.7°C in rural areas, resulting in a UHI intensity of 7.2°C.
